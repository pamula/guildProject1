Restaurant Description

A quality and relaxing rural country pub and restaurant, run by award winning owners, in the beautiful Utah village just a short drive from the alpine and snowbird . The building and decor are charming and rustic, with elegant modern touches. Relax by the cozy log burner perfect for winter evenings or enjoy outdoor seating overlooking beautiful mountains or in a pretty courtyard filled with flowers in the summer months. A daily menu, all freshly prepared in the pub kitchen, includes fresh meat, pies, vegetables and chef's daily specials with main course prices ranging from $6.50 - $19.50. As all dishes are prepared in the pub kitchen, they are able to cater for most dietary needs, with most sauces, soups and gravy all created gluten free. The Bhukti also offers gluten free bread and gluten free Utah's puddings on Sundays. Step in and enjoy our food with family and friends.
